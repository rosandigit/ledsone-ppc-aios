# intelligence-inbox

## Purpose
Entry point for raw, unprocessed inputs before they are reviewed and filed elsewhere in the repository.

## What belongs here
- New observations, alerts and external inputs awaiting triage
- Items in the sub-folders below, moved to `processed/` once handled

## What should NOT be stored here
- Anything already filed as evidence, a report or a decision
- Final or approved documents
